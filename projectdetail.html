Government Agency Homepage & Navigation Redesign - Department of Education

Problem

The current website of the U.S Department of Education does not have a useful navigational menu and many landing pages are not accessible except through the FAQs section. Many people find it very difficult to use. The mobile version of the homepage is not responsive, many component can't be selected without zooming in.

Solution

A redesign of the navigation menu, desktop and mobile homepage.

Timeline

3 Weeks

What I Did

Analyze and conduct research with existing design as a team then redesign, test a prototype individually, then iterate base on user feedback.
