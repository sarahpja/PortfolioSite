Hi, I'm Sarah. I'm a mechanical engineer turned UX designer. I am passionate about making things, learning about them, and solving problems. I would love to create user-centered designs that not only look great but also enhance the learning experience for users. Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy solving puzzles, playing Dungeons and Dragons, and engaging in other intellectually stimulating activities. These hobbies have helped me develop my problem-solving skills and creativity, which I believe are essential qualities for a successful UX designer.
